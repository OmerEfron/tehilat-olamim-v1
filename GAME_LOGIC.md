Game starts with 52 cards deck (no jokers)
max 9 players
for each player, the game goes like that: 
he needs to guess the next cards, in this order:
1st card - color (black or red)
2nd card - if the card is greater than the first card, below, or exactly the same (by number only (J=11, Q=12 etc.)). when its exact, its called "borders"
3nd card - if the next card is below the smaller card from the first two, above the biggest one, between them or "borders" (one of them)
4th card - the shape only
5th card - the number itself (no shape or color)

example of a win:
player's guess|card
black|Ace of Spades
below|5 of Hearts
between|7 of Clubs
clubs|9 of Clubs
Jack|Jack of Diamond


notice:
Ace is the biggest, 2 is the smallest.

when the deck is finished, if its in the middle of the round, keep those cards on and shuffle the rest
when a player wins, it means he got an Eternal Glory

hebrew translate:
Ace - אס
Jack - נסיך
Queen - מלכה
King - מלך

Eternal Glory - תהילת עולמים
